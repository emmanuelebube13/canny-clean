Frequently Asked Questions

What is professional bin cleaning?

Professional bin cleaning involves using high-pressure hot water and eco-friendly sanitizers to thoroughly clean, disinfect, and deodorize your garbage bins, removing dirt, bacteria, and odors effectively.

How often should I have my bins cleaned?

We recommend cleaning your bins every 1-3 months, depending on usage and environmental factors, to maintain hygiene and prevent pest infestations.

Are your cleaning products eco-friendly?

Yes, we use biodegradable, non-toxic cleaning agents that are safe for the environment, with no harmful chemicals entering the waterways.

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve the Halifax Regional Municipality (HRM), including Halifax, Dartmouth, and surrounding communities.
