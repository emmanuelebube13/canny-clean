Our Story & Commitment to Clean

Mission: Hygienic Bins, Healthier Communities

BIOBINPROS was founded right here in the **Halifax Regional Municipality (HRM)** with a simple yet critical mission: to eliminate the unhygienic conditions found in everyday garbage, recycling, and organic bins. We believe that a clean bin is a vital step toward a clean, odor-free home and a healthier community.

Our Eco-Friendly Values

We are deeply committed to the environment. Unlike DIY cleaning that allows contaminated runoff into storm drains, our custom-built cleaning unit captures all wastewater. We use **100% biodegradable, high-pressure hot water** and sanitizing agents to disinfect your bins without harming the planet.

Service Area: Right to Your Doorstep

We proudly serve both **residential and commercial** clients across the entire HRM, including **Halifax, Dartmouth, Bedford, Sackville**, and surrounding communities. If your bins get picked up, we can clean them!
